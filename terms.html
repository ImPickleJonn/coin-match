Coin Match — Terms of Use

Last updated: 2026-05-20

What this is

Coin Match is a free-to-play match-3 game. You can play forever without spending anything. In-game purchases use Telegram Stars and are processed by Telegram.

Virtual currency

"Coins", "Gems", and any other in-game currency or item have no real-world value, are non-transferable, and cannot be exchanged for cash. Purchases are final and non-refundable except where required by law or Telegram's own refund policy.

No gambling

Coin Match is a skill-based puzzle game. Coins earned and bet inside the game are virtual and have no cash value. The "bet" mechanic is a multiplier choice — you cannot win or withdraw real money.

Acceptable use

Don't try to break, cheat, or exploit the game. Don't impersonate, abuse, or harass other players in any in-game feature. We reserve the right to suspend accounts violating these terms.
